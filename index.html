Excaly Enrichment Data Software

Find leads from over +30M B2B companies. This tool gets leads from a list of companies or domains. It is perfect for B2B companies, Sales and Growth Hackers. The best enrich software to find leads. Ideal for generating leads to prospect them by cold email. Find business emails and leads information. Export leads directly to Excel with business data.

Excaly Email, Phone & Social Media Extractor

From a list of uploaded domains, this tool extracts emails, social media URLs and usernames (Facebook, Twitter, etc.), phone numbers on all pages of each website. A report is available to download as Excel once all the emails are extracted. This is the best solution to extract all emails from a list of websites (companies).

Excaly Email Finder

This tool will try to find the email of each contact from any uploaded csv containing the first name, last name, and company name (or domain) (for example, first.last@company.com). A perfect tool for discovering emails from a list of business data.

Excaly Domain Reserve

Our domain reserve gets a daily dump of all domains created on the Internet, that means all new companies. This tool produces around +100K leads of new domains created each day with location, phone numbers, emails, domains, registered date, and more. It's perfect for capturing new companies created on the Internet and generate new leads from it by selling SEO, marketing services, WordPress websites, development or content creation, etc.

Excaly Online Review Management Software

With this tool, you can install a simple widget on your websites to increase reviews and force your visitors to put a review about your website. It's perfect for any business or local business to increase its customers reviews. It works with Google, Yelp, and Facebook reviews. This technology helps to increase real organic reviews by driving visitors to the review page.

Excaly Chatbot Solution

Convert your visitors into leads. Automatically chat with your visitors and capture data from them. With this tool, you will be able to install a chatbot to capture information from your website visitors. The collected leads information could be sent by email, SMS (mobile number), or directly into a Slack channel.

Excaly Sales Sequence

A simple software to create emailing or sequence campaigns. Make new customers by sending newsletters or cold emails (send emails until recipients reply) by adding their own SMTP provider and IMAP. Track opens, clicks, bounce, unsubscribe, replies and create nice emailing campaigns in plain text or HTML. This tool gives you the power to do emailing campaigns at scale with high volume. Design nice newsletters or simple cold emails with the simple online editor.

Excaly Fake Emails Detection Api

Each time someone registers to your service, this API to verifies if the email is correct and not a disposable email to prevent fake registration.

Excaly Social Proof Widget

This tool generates and displays notifications on your website to show random messages to your visitors. This will increase your sales and credibility

Excaly B2B Social Media Email Extractor

Extract emails and contacts from B2B social media. Find new leads in one click and create targeted lists. Our system will help you to find leads from top social media platforms based on job title, location, country, industry, and company size.
